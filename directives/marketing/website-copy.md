## How It Works

**Section headline:** How It Works

**Section subheadline:** The Five Step Process to Building Your Audience.

**Step 1: Discovery call**
A 30 minute call where we learn your product and audience.

**Step 2: Onboarding deep-dive**
We do a full product walkthrough, voice sample, competitor research, and start mapping your Month 1 content strategy. Takes about an hour.

**Step 3: You record clips**
Twice a month, we send a filming brief with specific clips. Takes you 30 minutes to an hour each time.

**Step 4: Production and Delivery**
Strategy, scripting, editing, captions, scheduling, and posting. Content is delivered across every platform. One approval step and revision round.

**Step 5: Monthly Recap**
A 30-minute call breaking down the last month's performance and strategizing for the next month.

**Won't AI-generated content feel inauthentic to my audience?**
83% of consumers can spot pure AI video, and 36% say it lowers trust. We know. That's why Wraith produces founder-led, human content. AI accelerates production (editing, visuals, scheduling) but the content itself is you. That's what cuts through.

Most founders record a few clips with no scripting, no hooks, and no strategy, then conclude content doesn't work for them. The problem wasn't that you posted. It's that nothing was optimized. Strategy and quality are the difference between content that performs and content that sits there.
